How to Play the Dice Game

Objective: Be the first player to reach or exceed a score of 30 points.

Setup:

  1. The game has two players, Player 1 and Player 2.
  2. Both players start with a score of 0.
  3. Player 1 starts the game.

Gameplay:

  1. Rolling the Dice:
    • Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll a six-sided dice.
    • The result of the dice roll will be displayed.
  2. If the dice shows any number except 1:
    • The rolled number is added to your current score.
    • You can choose to roll the dice again or hold your score.
  3. If the dice shows 1:
    • Your current score is reset to 0.
    • It becomes the other player's turn.
  4. Holding the Score:
    • Click the "Hold" button to add your current score to your total score.
    • Your turn ends, and it becomes the other player's turn.

Winning the Game:

  1. The first player to reach or exceed a total score of 30 points wins the game.
  2. The winning player will be highlighted.

Starting a New Game:

  1. Click the "New Game" button to reset all scores and start a new game.
  2. Player 1 will start the new game.

Notes:

  • The current active player is highlighted.
  • The dice is hidden at the start and reappears when rolled.
  • Use the "New Game" button at any time to reset the game.
